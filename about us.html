School History

Established in the heart of Khiwansar, our school has been a center of learning and development for over two decades. We take pride in nurturing young minds and fostering an environment where students thrive academically and personally.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower every student with the knowledge, values, and skills necessary to become responsible citizens and global leaders.

Mission: To provide quality education through holistic development, moral guidance, and inclusive learning practices.
