Admission Process

Admissions for classes 6 to 12 are open every academic year between April and June.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid transfer certificate from previous school
  • Birth certificate for age verification
  • Recent passport-size photographs

How to Apply

Admission forms are available at the school office. Parents/guardians can visit between 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM on working days.
